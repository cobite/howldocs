Posting and staking

How to post​

  1. Click on Post at the bottom of the left pane on the Howl app

  1. Type what you want to post in the blank space and click on Post.

  1. Approve the Keplr transaction prompt

How to stake​

  1. Find a post on Howl social you want to stake your Howl to

Note: you can stake to more than 1 post at a time.

  1. Click on the heart/like icon below the post:

  1. Type the amount of Howl you want to stake and click Stake. Then approve the transaction request
