Getting Started & FAQ

Social for the Interchain

Howl is built on Cosmos, making it fast and secure. After launch, it will have integrations with web2 socials. Post on Howl, and have the post broadcast elsewhere.

DAOs by Default

We support DAOs and multi-sigs as first-class citizens. Want to post signed messages as a DAO or multi-sig? We've got you covered. Comment on and reference DAODAO proposals out-of-the-box.

Support Creators

Like content? Your likes and staking support the creators you enjoy. Want to make it regular? Howl Pay makes repeated payments easy.
